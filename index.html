Awakening For Earth

Facing the Climate Crisis

Who We are

We are a welcoming community of spiritual practitioners who envisions a just and livable earth for all life. We are dedicated to facing the climate crisis and the suffering that has ensued with compassion and determination to engage in nonviolent direct action. We see climate justice within the framework of social justice and commit to advocating for policies that empower communities most impacted by climate change.

What We Do

We invoke our mindfulness practice to hold the pain of this ecological and spiritual crisis.

We bring silent sitting and walking meditation to public spaces to raise awareness to the devastating effects of our planetary emergency and the urgent need to take personal and collective action.

In alliance with other spiritual practitioners and ecoactivist organizations, we participate in events and direct actions to bring about change in social, political, and economic sectors.

We forge mutually supportive relationships with like-minded and like-spirited individuals and groups through networking and sharing information & resources.

How We Sustain Ourselves and Our Vision

Recognizing the need for regeneration in this consuming effort, we invite pauses to be in community, to care for one another and nourish ourselves with spiritual practice, food, music, and art to bring the energy of love and understanding to sustain this demanding effort.
